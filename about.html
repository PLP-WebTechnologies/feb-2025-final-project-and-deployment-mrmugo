What is Fukú?

Fukú is a mythical curse described in Dominican folklore. It was popularized in Junot Díaz’s novel *The Brief Wondrous Life of Oscar Wao*. The curse is said to haunt families for generations, often tied to the legacy of colonialism and dictatorship.
